Company Data Rules:

Required columns for sales transactions:
- transaction_id (must be a unique string)
- customer_email (must be a valid email address)
- purchase_amount (must be a positive number, no negative values)
- purchase_date (must be in YYYY-MM-DD format)

Additional quality rules:
- No duplicate rows
- No missing values in any column
- Emails must contain '@' and a domain
- purchase_amount cannot be negative
